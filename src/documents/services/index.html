Services

Human Resources and Placement Services

Employee and job placement interface for medical and industrial trade industries internationally. We bring both experienced and inexperienced applicants together with industries seeking growth or assistance within their work force.

Ecological recycling of commercial solid waste (MS)

Working directly with receiving stations and smelting operations to absorb a spectrum of metals for recycling. The capacity to take in 150,000 tons monthly of any grade solid metal waste.

Commodities and Textiles Trading Post

We operate at ports of entry in the South Pacific Ocean for the delivery of fabricated or raw commercial materials, residential, and finished goods for construction development purposes. Partnered with freight forwarding and logistics agents.

Project Finance and Development Consulting

A member of a Global consortium for assisting at all levels of project and business development. We have to ability to utilize private sector funding as well Government grant programs through Lobby group associations. With a tenure of over 30+ years of experience in international commercial, residential, and corporate development and construction expertise.

Aquaculture Technology and Feed Production

Ownership of commercial scale fisheries, utilizing green technologies for organically farmed and sustainable open sea cultivation. Implementing new technologies to produce feed for several varieties of species, both for aquaculture and livestock farmers.
